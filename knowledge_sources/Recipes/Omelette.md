**Quick Omelette Recipe (1 serving)**

**Ingredients**

- 2–3 eggs
- Salt and black pepper
- 1 tbsp butter or oil
- Optional fillings: cheese, ham, mushrooms, onions, spinach, tomatoes


**Steps**

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt and pepper, and whisk briefly until combined.
    
2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
    
3. Pour in the eggs and gently swirl the pan to spread them evenly.
    
4. When the edges begin to set (about 30–60 seconds), add any fillings to one half.
    
5. Once mostly set but still slightly soft on top, fold the omelette in half.
    
6. Cook another 30–60 seconds, then slide onto a plate and serve immediately.
